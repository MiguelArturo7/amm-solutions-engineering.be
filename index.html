A fresh perspective on engineering

AMM Solution Engineering helps clients with their challenges — whatever form those take. That might mean using microcontrollers to read and visualise data from industrial sensors, or it might mean something more hands-on: working at heights, carrying out preventive or corrective maintenance, or finding a simple fix to a recurring problem. The goal is always the same — to understand what you actually need, and to help you get there.

How I work

My approach

I believe in honest communication and realistic expectations. As a one-person operation, I focus on doing things thoughtfully rather than overpromising. Every engagement starts with listening.

1

Listen & understand

I take time to understand your setup, your constraints, and what you actually need — before suggesting anything.

2

Propose clearly

I lay out options in plain language with honest cost and effort estimates — no jargon, no hidden complexity.

3

Build carefully

I develop, test, and iterate with care — keeping you informed at each stage and adapting when needed.

4

Hand over well

Delivery includes documentation and a proper handover so you can maintain and build on what was created.
