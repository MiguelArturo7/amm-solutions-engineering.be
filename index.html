Sensors alone do nothing. Data alone does nothing. The value is in the interpretation — understanding what the numbers mean for your specific equipment and turning that into a signal you can act on.

§02 What it prevents
01 — DOWNTIME
Unplanned production stops

A vibration anomaly caught three weeks early is a scheduled maintenance window. Caught after the fact, it is a production halt at the worst possible moment.

02 — ENERGY
Invisible energy loss

Slow thermal drift rarely triggers an alarm — until the invoice arrives. Continuous monitoring makes it visible before it compounds.

03 — COMPLIANCE
Regulatory exposure

An out-of-range reading in a controlled environment can carry consequences. Knowing in real time gives you a documented response — not a gap in your records.

04 — REPAIR
Emergency call-out costs

The most expensive repair is always the one that could not wait. Predictive signals let you plan on your schedule — not on failure's schedule.

§03 Process
1
Understand your installation

I visit your site, identify critical assets, and determine what actually needs to be monitored and why. No standard packages — only what makes sense for your specific situation.

2
Install & integrate

I wire and configure the sensors myself and connect them to a microcontroller that reads them continuously. Hardware to firmware — done in one intervention.

3
Define normal & set alert logic

I establish baseline behaviour for your equipment and configure thresholds and triggers that produce real, actionable warnings — not just numbers on a screen.

4
Live dashboard + ongoing support

You get a live view of your key metrics. When something drifts outside normal bounds, you are alerted before it becomes a problem. I remain available to interpret, adjust, and expand.

§04 What I monitor

Examples of sensor types I work with — the list is not fixed. Every installation starts from your specific assets and what actually needs to be watched.

TMP

Temperature

Catches thermal drift on motors, switchgear, and storage before it becomes damage.

→ Overheating / thermal runaway / spoilage
HUM

Humidity

Detects moisture before it corrodes contacts, degrades insulation, or breaches compliance.

→ Corrosion / insulation failure / non-compliance
VIB

Vibration

Monitors amplitude and pattern in rotating machinery — not just whether something shakes.

→ Bearing failure / imbalance / loosening
ACC

Tilt & acceleration

6-axis IMU detects structural shift, impact events, or orientation change before visual inspection.

→ Structural drift / g-force event / mounting failure
LUX

Light & occupancy

Flags energy waste, unexpected access, or environmental changes in sensitive zones.

→ Energy waste / unauthorised access
DST-S

Short distance

Object presence, part positioning, close-range level detection. Contactless, no wear.

→ Misalignment / presence / fill level
DST-L

Long distance

Tank levels, silo fill height, gate or barrier status over longer ranges. Continuous, non-contact.

→ Overflow / empty tank / intrusion
